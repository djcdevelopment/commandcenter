- **VM networking needs the original MAC pinned** (`00155D0CC700`, recovered from the old `.vmcx` via `strings`) or the Linux guest won't DHCP. Per-VM, recover each from its `.vmcx`.

- Jaeger v1 is EOL — works now, plan a v2 migration later.
